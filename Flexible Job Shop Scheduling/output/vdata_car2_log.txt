-----

the makespan, so the makespan-critical job deviates by re-routing or
re-sequencing its critical operations; a deviation is kept only if the

a Nash equilibrium (a critical-path local optimum). Iterated kicks and
many random restarts (keeping the best run) escape weak equilibria.
